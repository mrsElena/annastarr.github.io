Artist

ILONA OBOLENSKA is a painter who is originally from Ukraine. After graduating from Kryvyi Rih National University, with a bachelors of science degree in Industrial Engineering Ilona set herself on a new path towards her life dream. As a women from Eastern Europe, her life has seen many life perspectives and adventures. Art has been her compass to find the meaning of her life.

The city of New York has been her main inspiration to follow her dream. Ilona had the privilege of being trained at the New York Academy of Art, where she graduated in 2017. Her work and skills are in spray art, oil, landscapes of cities, and architecture. Her style is uniquely mixed with spray art and oil to create the canvas into a special piece of meaning.

Art is a need for my soul. The need for beauty and creativity that embodies it is inseparable from man, and without it a person, perhaps, would not want to live in the world
